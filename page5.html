DATA ANALYSIS AND ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE 

At Hea.It t we harnesses vast amounts of clinical and patient data to deliver highly accurate predictive clinical analytics. Our analytics team will identify relationships between real-time physiological data and latent medical conditions, recognizing any changes in the patient’s condition.

We can develop a proprietary machine-learning prediction tool that identifies and quantifies an individual’s risk for developing chronic disease. This risk stratification is critical for early intervention and effective resource allocation by healthcare providers . Identifying individuals at higher risk of developing chronic disease will allow tailored intervention and drive patients to action. The interventional communication and disease management will allow patients to take care of their own health

We can continuously optimizes patient’s interactions, goals, and messages, to fit the patient’s daily life and real-world capabilities, in a way that increases probability of action.
